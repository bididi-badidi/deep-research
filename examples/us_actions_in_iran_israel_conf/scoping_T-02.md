# Scoping for Task T-02

**Sub-question:** What are the official US diplomatic and economic actions regarding the Iran-Israel conflict from April 2024 to the present?

**Specific answers looking for:**
- Dates and details of new sanctions against Iran, its military (IRGC), or proxies (Hezbollah, Houthis, etc.).
- UN Security Council resolutions, votes, and vetoes involving the US regarding Iran or Israel.
- Key diplomatic visits/summits by US officials (e.g., Secretary Blinken, Amos Hochstein) related to de-escalation or ceasefire.
- Specific ceasefire mediation milestones (e.g., the August 2024 proposal).

**Complete answer looks like:**
A chronological list of actions with clear dates, specific entities sanctioned (if applicable), and citations for each.

**Likely Sources:**
- US Department of State (Press releases, Fact sheets)
- US Department of the Treasury (OFAC announcements)
- UN Security Council Meeting Records
- Major news outlets (Reuters, AP) for real-time reporting of summits and mediation steps.

**Search Terms:**
- "US sanctions Iran April 2024"
- "US Treasury Iran sanctions proxies 2024"
- "US UN Security Council Israel Iran resolution April 2024"
- "Blinken Middle East travel 2024 timeline"
- "US ceasefire mediation Iran Israel August 2024"

**Out of scope:**
- Domestic US protests or congressional debates.
- Israeli or Iranian domestic politics.
- Purely military movements (unless part of a diplomatic package).
