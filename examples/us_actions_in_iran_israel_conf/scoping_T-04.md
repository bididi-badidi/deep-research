# Scoping - Task T-04: Global Consequences of US Actions (Apr 2024-Present)

## Sub-question
What are the global consequences (diplomatic, economic/trade, geopolitical) of US military, diplomatic, and economic actions in the Iran-Israel conflict since April 2024?

## Scope
- **Timeframe:** April 1, 2024, to Present.
- **Geography:** International (EU, Arab states, Global trade routes).
- **Inclusions:** EU/Arab state responses, Red Sea shipping impacts, geopolitical realignments.
- **Exclusions:** US domestic impacts, Global oil prices.

## Research Strategy
1. Identify EU and G7 policy shifts (sanctions, diplomatic pressure).
2. Analyze Arab state participation in regional defense (April 13-14) and subsequent diplomatic balancing.
3. Assess Red Sea shipping volume changes and economic impacts for non-US regions (EU/Asia).
4. Identify signs of BRICS+ or Sino-Russian involvement/beneficiaries of the conflict.

## Sources to look for
- EU Council press releases.
- G7 Leaders' Communiqués.
- IMF PortWatch/UNCTAD maritime reports.
- News reports on IMEC status.
- Analysis from IISS or CFR on regional security architecture.
